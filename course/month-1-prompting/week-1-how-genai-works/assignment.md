# Assignment: Context and Temperature Exploration

## Task
Write a short report documenting your experiments with temperature and context.

## Requirements
1. Run the same prompt at 3 different temperature levels (0, 0.7, 1.5)
2. Run one prompt with and without additional context
3. For each run, note:
   - The exact prompt used
   - The temperature setting
   - The output
   - Your observation (what changed?)

## Deliverable
A document (Google Doc or Markdown) with your findings. Minimum 300 words.

## Grading Criteria
- All 5 experiments documented (3 temps + 2 context)
- Observations are specific, not vague ("output was more random" is too vague — what kind of random?)
- At least one insight about how you'll use temperature in your work
