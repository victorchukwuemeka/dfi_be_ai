# Assignment: Creative Campaign Package

## Task
Develop a complete creative campaign package for a real or hypothetical product/initiative.

## Requirements
1. Choose a product, service, or initiative to promote
2. Produce:
   - **10 brainstormed ideas** with AI ranking
   - **3 messaging options** (problem, benefit, emotion focus)
   - **1 full campaign concept** with: core idea, tagline, channel strategy, success metric
   - **3 pieces of content** (e.g., social post, email subject line, headline)
3. Show at least 2 refinement rounds
4. Document what AI contributed vs your own thinking

## Deliverable
A document with the full campaign package, showing the creative process from brainstorm to final output.

## Grading Criteria
- 10 ideas generated and ranked (20%)
- 3 distinct messaging angles (20%)
- Full campaign concept is coherent and actionable (25%)
- 3 content pieces included (15%)
- Refinement process documented (10%)
- Honest reflection on AI vs human contribution (10%)
