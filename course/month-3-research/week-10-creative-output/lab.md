# Lab: Structured Creativity

## Objective
Generate creative content using AI with structured constraints.

## Instructions

### Part 1: Brainstorm
1. Pick a topic relevant to your work (or a hypothetical product/initiative)
2. Use the brainstorming prompt pattern to generate 10 ideas
3. Ask AI to rank them by novelty, feasibility, and impact
4. Pick the top 3

### Part 2: Develop Messaging
1. Take one of the top 3 ideas
2. Use the messaging framework prompt to generate 3 message angles
3. Pick the strongest angle

### Part 3: Flesh Out
1. Take the chosen angle
2. Ask AI to expand it into a one-page campaign concept
3. Refine with at least 2 constraints (e.g., budget, timeline)

### Part 4: Compare with Human-Only Approach
1. Before using AI, quickly sketch your own ideas
2. Compare: did AI suggest something you wouldn't have thought of?

## Deliverables
- All 10 brainstormed ideas with AI ranking
- 3 messaging options
- The expanded campaign concept (before and after refinement)
- Comparison: AI vs your own initial ideas
