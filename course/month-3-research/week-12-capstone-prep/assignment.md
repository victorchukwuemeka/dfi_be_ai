# Assignment: Capstone Proposal

## Task
Submit a one-page capstone proposal for review.

## Requirements
Cover:
1. **Problem statement** (1-2 paragraphs)
   - What is the current pain point?
   - Who experiences it?
   - What does it cost (time, quality, effort)?

2. **Proposed workflow** (4-6 steps)
   - High-level steps
   - What role does AI play in each?
   - Where is human judgment required?

3. **Expected impact** (before/after)
   - Time estimate before vs after
   - Quality estimate before vs after
   - Any other measurable dimension

4. **Success criteria**
   - How will you know the workflow is better?
   - What specific metric needs to improve?

## Deliverable
A `capstone/proposal.md` file in your repository with the complete proposal.

## Grading Criteria
- Problem is specific and real (25%)
- Workflow is well-defined with clear AI and human roles (25%)
- Before/after impact is quantified or at least estimated (25%)
- Success criteria are specific and measurable (25%)
