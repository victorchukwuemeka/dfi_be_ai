# Assignment: Verified Research Brief

## Task
Produce a research brief on a topic of your choice, with explicit verification of claims.

## Requirements
1. Choose a topic (professional or general interest)
2. Follow the full workflow:
   - Scope the topic with AI
   - Refine research questions
   - Gather information
   - Require confidence ratings on all claims
   - Cross-check at least 2 claims manually
   - Document accuracy of AI vs manual check
3. Produce a final research brief (500-800 words) with:
   - Key findings
   - Confidence rating for each
   - Gaps and uncertainties
   - Sources (even if AI-generated)

## Deliverable
A document containing:
- Scoping output
- Refined questions
- Initial research output
- Cross-check results
- Manual verification notes
- Final research brief with confidence ratings

## Grading Criteria
- Full workflow followed and documented (30%)
- Confidence ratings on all claims (20%)
- Manual verification of at least 2 claims (20%)
- Final brief is well-structured and usable (15%)
- Reflection on verification process (15%)
