# Lab: Research and Verify

## Objective
Practice the full research workflow: scope → question → gather → verify → document.

## Instructions

### Part 1: Scope a Topic
1. Choose a topic you know little about
2. Use AI to break it into 3-5 sub-questions
3. Refine the top 2 questions

### Part 2: Gather Information
1. Ask AI the refined questions
2. Ask for confidence ratings on each claim
3. Identify the 3 claims with lowest confidence

### Part 3: Verify
1. Take the lowest-confidence claim and ask AI to elaborate on sources
2. Do a manual check (quick web search) on one claim
3. Document what you found

### Part 4: Cross-Check
Ask the same core question 3 different ways. Compare the 3 answers for consistency.

## Deliverables
- Scoping output with refined questions
- Initial research with confidence ratings
- Verification results (what was accurate? what was wrong?)
- Cross-check comparison
- What surprised you about the accuracy?
