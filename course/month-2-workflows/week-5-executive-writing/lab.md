# Lab: Transform Raw Notes into Executive Output

## Objective
Turn messy notes into 3 different professional formats.

## Instructions

### Part 1: Gather Raw Notes
Write 5-10 messy bullet points about a recent project, meeting, or idea. Include fragments, incomplete thoughts, and mixed priorities.

### Part 2: Three Formats
Use an AI tool to transform those notes into:

1. **A memo** addressed to your manager or team
2. **A briefing document** for someone who missed the meeting
3. **An email** proposing next steps in 3 sentences

### Part 3: Refine
Pick the weakest output and refine it with specific constraints:
- "Shorten by 50%"
- "Change tone from formal to conversational"
- "Add a specific ask at the end"

## Deliverables
- Original raw notes
- All 3 formatted outputs
- The refined version of the weakest output
- What pattern worked best for your content type?
