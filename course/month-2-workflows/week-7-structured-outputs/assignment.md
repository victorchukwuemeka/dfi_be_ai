# Assignment: Structured Decision Package

## Task
Use AI to produce a structured decision package for a real decision you face.

## Requirements
Choose a real decision (or realistic scenario) and produce:

1. **Decision brief** — 1-paragraph summary of the decision needed
2. **Comparison table** — at least 3 options, 4+ criteria
3. **Scored ranking** — weighted scores with rationale
4. **Action plan** — the recommended option broken into steps with owners and deadlines
5. **Risk matrix** — at least 4 risks with mitigations

All outputs must be generated by AI from a single narrative input (your description of the situation).

## Deliverable
A document with:
- Situation description (the input you gave the AI)
- All 5 structured outputs
- At least 2 refinements that improved output quality
- Note: which output format was most helpful for your decision?

## Grading Criteria
- All 5 output types produced
- Outputs are coherent and internally consistent (same decision, same options)
- Refinements show clear improvement
- Real or realistic scenario
- Reflection on usefulness of each format
