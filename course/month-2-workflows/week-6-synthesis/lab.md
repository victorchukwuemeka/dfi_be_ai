# Lab: Synthesize from Multiple Sources

## Objective
Take 3+ related inputs and synthesize them into actionable output.

## Instructions

### Part 1: Gather Sources
Collect 3-4 related pieces of content:
- Meeting notes
- Slack/email threads
- Reports or articles
- Customer feedback

### Part 2: Extract and Synthesize
1. Feed all sources to an AI tool with a synthesis prompt
2. Review the output — does it find connections you missed?
3. Refine: ask for specific patterns (disagreements, gaps, action items)

### Part 3: Decision
Based on the synthesis, ask the AI to recommend a decision:
"Based on this synthesis, what is the single most important action to take? Why?"

## Deliverables
- Raw sources (can be redacted)
- Synthesis output
- Refined synthesis (targeting a specific pattern)
- The recommended decision
- Did the AI identify something you would have missed?
